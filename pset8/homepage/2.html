Why Computer Science?

Computer Science is defined as the "study of computers and computational devices", and has always been around. The only thing is that people haven't always called it as such.
Its story goes back to the 1600's, with the mechanical calculator, in 1623. To know that shows just how old this topic is. And there is even more to it.
